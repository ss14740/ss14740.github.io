D2 in the Summer

This short film follows Fatima, a student at NYUAD and her journey to the dining hall. She is caught outside on a hot summer's day, and wishes to have a refresher at D2. However, she is dismayed to find it closed. This film was shot and recorded on an iPhone, and is under a minute long.

Boredom to Adventure

This interactive digital comic follows Tim, a young boy who encounters a time travel machine and discovers different eras in time. The comic is a choose your own adventure game, where the viewer is able to choose to go to the past or future, and click levers that determine the next era they end up.

Journey Through the Forest

This audio oriented website simulates an immersive forest walk as the day turns to night. All screens have a play, pause, and volume button. The central button takes the user through the journey, allowing them to go forward at their own pace.

Hunt for Water

This is a short film that tells the story of a young man and his search for water. He is a student in NYUAD, and is uncomfortable thirsty under the hot desert sun. He walks to the convenience store for water, but encounters many obstacles, all the while the viewer hears his inner monologue.

BlackMagic Pocket Camera

At NYUAD, this camera can be found in the art center. After a training session, the camera can be rented and used. In the set, there are three different lenses, and the camera can be handheld or placed on a tripod. "Hunt for Water" film was shot on a this camera, mainly using the 35mm lens.

Adobe

Adobe has many softwares. I focused on Premier Pro and Photoshop. The former is a video and audio editing software, and the latter is a photo design software. Both have a seven day free trial. The panels in "Boredome to Adventure" were created using Photoshop, and "Hunt for Water" was edited on Premier Pro.

H5 Zoom Recorder

This audio recorder can be rented from the arts center at NYUAD. The set comes with headphones and assorted recording equipment. The audio device is handheld. The audio from "Hunt for Water" and parts of the audio in "Journey Through the Forest" were recorded with this recorder.
