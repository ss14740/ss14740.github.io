Directed by Denis Villneuve, Blade Runner 2049 tells the story of Officer K and his love for Joi, who is an android and a hologram. K tracks down rogue androids, and on his journey, uncovers a shocking secret.

Upon his realization of the truth, Officer K attempts to track down Rick Deckard, a former blade runner. He is able to bring Joi's hologram with him, in a pen form, and this means she can be 'like a real girl'.
