D2 in the Summer follow Fatima, a student at NYUAD in the summertime. She is walking towards the dining hall, also known as D2, and contemplates purchasing an acai, a summer refresher. However, upon reaching the door, she discovers it has closed for the summer. Disheartened, she walks away into the blazing heat, surrounded by palm trees and the picturesque scenery of campus.

Fatima, Aisha, and Sunny worked collaboratively on this short film. They chose the subject of D2, as it is the most frequented dining hall on campus, and its closure resulted in the surprise and dismay of many. Fatima stars as the student who we follow. The creators used many still shots of nature, such as the greenery, sky, and cat. They showed the serene nature of campus, and contrasted it with the dining hall inconvenience.
